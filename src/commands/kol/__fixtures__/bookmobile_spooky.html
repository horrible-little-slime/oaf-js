The Bookmobile

"Well, hello there," says the scruffy man standing in the window of the bookmobile.

"I've got a lot of wonderful books! So many wonderful books! In fact, this week, I've got quite a few copies of the Pocket Guide to Mild Evil. You are going to want you to read this book, so get it before I come to my senses. "

"I want to get rid of these, so I'm going to keep lowering the price as people buy them. It's a sound economic principle. For you the price is 9,932,000 Meat! I'm heading out in 9 days or whenever these books are gone."
